FlowAuvis: Efficient audio visual speech enhancement models using a conditional flow matching


Anonymous*
Under review

Abstract

The goal of this study is to extract target speech from a mixture of sounds. While previous diffusion-based methods have exhibited exceptional quality, they suffer from heavy computations and slow inference speeds. To address this issue, in this paper, we present FlowAuVis, which is structured considering efficiency and can generate clearly isolated target speech. Specifically, we leverages a conditional flow matching algorithm with a single sampling step. Additionally, we streamline the inherent U-net architecture of previous diffusion models to further enhance efficiency. Experimental results indicate that our framework achieves an inference speed approximately 22 times faster than our baseline diffusion model, demonstrating outstanding performance. Our findings highlight the exceptional suitability of FlowAuVis for speech enhancement tasks and real-world scenarios, effectively balancing accuracy and speed.
